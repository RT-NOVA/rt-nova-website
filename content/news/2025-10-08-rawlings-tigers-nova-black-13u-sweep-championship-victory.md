The Rawlings Tigers NOVA – Black 13U delivered an impressive showing on Sunday, earning back-to-back wins in dramatic and decisive fashion.

In the first matchup, the Tigers battled through a high-scoring contest against the Virginia Vols, securing a thrilling 10–9 walk-off victory. After jumping out to an early six-run lead, the Tigers showed resilience when the Vols rallied late to tie the game, ultimately reclaiming the lead in the bottom of the fifth to seal the win. Strong baserunning and disciplined at-bats were key, as the team collected 11 walks and stole 12 bases in a balanced offensive effort.

The Tigers carried that momentum into Game Two, defeating the Richmond Braves 12–6 behind another powerful offensive performance. The lineup consistently applied pressure with extra-base hits and timely run production, while the pitching staff combined to hold the Braves in check. The team’s defense added to the effort, turning a double play and limiting scoring opportunities throughout.

With solid teamwork, patience at the plate, and aggressive play all around, the Tigers capped off the championship with a 2–0 record and another strong step forward in tournament play.
