The Rawlings Tigers NOVA 12U continued their impressive season with a commanding run to claim the Stafford May Day Championship this weekend. Going undefeated throughout the tournament, the Tigers lit up the scoreboard with an explosive 33 total hits over 3 games. Backed by red-hot bats, dominant pitching, and sharp all-around play, they proved to be the team to beat — and no one could. Congratulations to the champions on a flawless and well-earned title run!

Unfortunately, alongside this impressive accomplishment comes some bittersweet news: Eason Anness will be departing the team due to his family’s relocation to Japan. The Tigers wish Eason all the best on this exciting new chapter and look forward to our paths crossing again in the hopefully not-too-distant future.
