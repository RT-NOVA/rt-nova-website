The Rawlings Tigers were proudly represented by six standout players at the prestigious Perfect Game All-State Tournament: Owen Trenkelbach, Parker Scisciani, Henry Bambling, Max Helchinger, Tyler Davidson, and Alex Medrano.

Owen Trenkelbach turned heads all weekend, playing a pivotal role in leading his team to a tournament championship. His red-hot bat was unstoppable, earning him the MVP award for hitting with a perfect batting average of 1.000—a rare and remarkable feat on such a big stage.

Meanwhile, Parker Scisciani, Henry Bambling, and Max Helchinger made impressive runs of their own, helping push their respective teams into the Silver Bracket. In an exciting semi-final matchup, teammates turned competitors as Parker and Max went head-to-head, with Parker’s team narrowly pulling out the win.

The intensity continued into the Silver Bracket championship, where **Parker** and **Henry** found themselves battling it out in a back-and-forth showdown. In a game filled with grit and determination, Parker’s team ultimately emerged victorious, capping off an incredible weekend of high-level baseball.

Congratulations to all six Tigers on their outstanding performances and for proudly representing the Rawlings Tigers program at the All-State level!
