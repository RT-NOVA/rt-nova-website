Rawlings Tigers NOVA – Black 13U turned in an outstanding weekend, finishing as the top seed in the bracket after back-to-back wins over Hit Doctor 13U, 10-1, and VSA Select DMV 13U Gold, 10-3. The Tigers carried that momentum into the championship game, where they battled hard in an exciting 11-10 contest.

The offense continued to shine in the title game, as the Tigers collected 10 hits and erupted for seven runs in the third inning to take control. Sebastian and Alex each recorded two hits, while Maximillian and Alex each drove in two runs. The team also showed patience at the plate with seven walks and stayed aggressive on the bases, stealing six bags on the day.

Rawlings Tigers NOVA – Black 13U also made big defensive plays, turning two double plays in the championship matchup. While the weekend ended just short of a title, the Tigers showed toughness, energy, and the ability to compete with anyone in the bracket. Earning the top seed and finishing runner-up capped off a strong all-around performance for the team.
