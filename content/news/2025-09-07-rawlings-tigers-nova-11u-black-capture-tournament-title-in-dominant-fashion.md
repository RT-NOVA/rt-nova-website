The Rawlings Tigers NOVA 11U Black stormed through the weekend to capture the championship, showcasing explosive offense, sharp defense, and lights-out pitching.

From the opening game, the Tigers made a statement—scoring 13 runs in the first inning on their way to a 19–3 victory over Ironbridge Olsen. Tigers relentlessly attacked at the plate, while the pitching staff set the tone with strikeouts and zero walks allowed.

The momentum was carried into the matchup against the Peninsula CoPilots, jumping out with eight runs in the first inning and never looking back in an 11–0 shutout win.

Over the course of the tournament, the Tigers outscored their opponents 30–3, proving their ability to combine aggressive hitting, smart baserunning, and clean defense.

This victory is a testament to the team’s hard work, determination, and true Tigers baseball spirit. Congratulations to the 11U Black squad and coaches on a well-deserved championship!
