Our Mission

Reducing barriers and supporting player growth.

The NOVA Tigers Booster Club supports and enhances the development of youth baseball players in Northern Virginia by reducing financial barriers, strengthening facilities, and building a strong community network around our athletes.

Your support helps create more opportunities for players and families throughout the Rawlings Tigers NOVA program.

Community Support Bring families, businesses, and supporters together around the athletes and teams representing Rawlings Tigers NOVA.
Tax-Deductible Giving The Booster Club is a 501(c)(3) organization. Donations are tax-deductible as allowed by law.
Program Impact Support player development, facilities, tournament travel, gear, safety, and community programs.
