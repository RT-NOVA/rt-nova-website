Program Overview

Northern Virginia travel baseball.

Rawlings Tigers NOVA serves youth baseball families across Northern Virginia, including Woodbridge, Dumfries, Montclair, Dale City, Stafford, Manassas, Fairfax, Prince William County, and surrounding communities.

The program is built for players and families looking for competitive travel baseball teams, structured development, professional coaching, and a strong team-first environment.

Player Development Purposeful skill work, baseball IQ, feedback, and growth goals that help players build confidence and performance.
Professional Coaching Experienced coaches focused on preparation, communication, mentorship, accountability, and teaching the game the right way.
Competitive Baseball Teams are prepared for strong local, regional, and national competition with a commitment to development and team standards.

Development, standards, and opportunity.
