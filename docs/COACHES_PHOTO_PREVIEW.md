# Coaches Page Photo Preview

Adds click-to-enlarge coach photo previews to the `/coaches/` page.

## Behavior

- Small coach photos subtly scale on hover/focus.
- Clicking or tapping a photo opens a larger preview.
- The preview includes the coach name and role/team context.
- Escape key, backdrop click, or close button closes the preview.

## Files

- `layouts/partials/page-coaches.html`
- `assets/css/coaches.css`

## Styling

Keep future styling scoped to `assets/css/coaches.css`.
